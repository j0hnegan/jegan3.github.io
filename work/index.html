New England Patriots

Digital Media for the NFL Football Dynasty

From the first sports team to have a website in 1995, to the first NFL team to reach a million followers on twitter, the New England Patriots have consistently been on the forefront of technology.

As a member of the Patriots Digital Media team, I specialized in front-end design and development for Kraft Sports sites such as Patriots.com, GilletteStadium.com and the New England Patriots ProShop.

In addition, I created graphics for the Patriots social channels, photographed and edited images from press conferences and locker room interviews, as well as built marketing emails which were sent to hundreds of thousands of inboxes on a weekly basis.
